Logo backing on dark themes

Both logos carry dark text/marks, so they need a light backing on dark — the goal is to make it elegant, not a hard white sticker. Same two real logos, four treatments. Click your favorite (mixing ideas is fine).

A · Current
Hard white card + 1px dark border. The dark border + sharp white rectangle is what reads as a sticker on dark.
B · Frosted lozenge
Soft near-white plate (subtle top-to-bottom gradient), no hard border, gentle drop shadow + inner light ring. Reads as an intentional plate, not a sticker. Most robust.
C · Feathered halo (your idea)
White fades to transparent at the edges — no rectangle at all, the backing melts into the dark bar. Most "gradient-y"; works best when the logo has breathing room around its marks.
D · Soft glow plate
Clean white plate with a faint cool glow diffusing into the bar — a little more "lit", keeps a crisp edge but loses the harsh border.
